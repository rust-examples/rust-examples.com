Comment Policy

Welcome to Rust Examples, a website and community focused on the Rust programming language.

We try our best to make sure that the discussions on our articles are troll, spam and abuse free but we’re not omnipresent. The comment section can be a great place for interaction with readers, writers and even for spawning stimulating conversation – it can be a great place for all of us! To ensure that you have a good time over here at Rust Examples, please make sure to stick to the following guidelines:

  • Be polite – don’t be rude to anyone and respect others. If something you say would get you in trouble offline, please don’t say it here. Remember there is a person behind the screen and that your words affect people.
  • Don’t troll. We appreciate and welcome criticism towards writers here, but comments that add no value to the discussion or that are merely insulting would be deleted without exception.
  • Spam? Goodbye. We understand that you can boost our SEO tenfold with this one trick, however, we are not interested. Please refrain from posting or we’ll give you a hand and show both you and your post the door.

To put it in a simple sentence, the guidelines boil down to “Be nice”. Follow these guidelines or our moderators will remove your comment and we may also ban you from participating in the discussions.

If you find any comments which violate our guidelines, please make sure to ‘flag’ or ‘mark as spam’ – our moderators highly appreciate your support!

Happy commenting!
